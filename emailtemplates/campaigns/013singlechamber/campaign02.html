SINGLE CHAMBER MACHINE

Product Description:

The Model DXDD21 intelligent tea bag packing machine is our newly developed heat-sealed, multifunctional and fully automatic machine. It is suitable for 20-60 mesh materials of non-viscous particles / broken tea / similar herbs in various industries such as medicine, health care, food etc. The machine adopts PLC, HMI and servo control so that it achieves advantage such as high intelligence, simple operation and convenient maintenance.
