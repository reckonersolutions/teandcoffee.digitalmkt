Sustainable Packaging Trends in FMCG

Increasing Sustainable Packaging: A Key Priority

Increasing the volume of sustainable packaging such as paper is the number one priority for improving the environmental performance of UK FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods) businesses – to avoid reputational and financial risk – according to a new study.

The FMCG sector is a significant contributor to countries’ economies. However, it is also one of the biggest creators of plastic waste, particularly in packaging.

Many companies in the FMCG sector have embraced sustainable packaging, and global brands have committed to pledges on targets to reduce conventional packaging and introduce more sustainable materials. However, many have yet to do so at scale and make the complete shift from conventional plastic to more sustainable packaging.

A new study from Birmingham, England-based Aquapak Polymers explores the future of flexible packaging in the sector, the materials likely to prevail, the timeframes for doing so, and the perceived barriers which must be overcome.
