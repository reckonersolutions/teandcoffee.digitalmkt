AI-based project to optimise coffee roasting

مشروع يعتمد على الذكاء الاصطناعي لتحسين تحميص القهوة

Brambati has launched an innovative AI-based project to optimise coffee roasting. The system uses artificial intelligence to monitor and adjust roasting parameters in real time, ensuring consistent quality and efficiency. This technology aims to help coffee producers achieve the perfect roast while reducing waste and energy consumption.
