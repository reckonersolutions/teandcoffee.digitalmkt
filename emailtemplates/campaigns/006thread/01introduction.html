About HMC Thread

HMC Thread, a trusted name in the industry since 2004, manufactures food-grade cotton thread and aluminum staple wire, used widely by tea factories around the world. With a production capacity of 30 tons per month, they ensure steady supply and exceptional quality. Their thread is compatible with a wide range of teabag packing machines, including:

  • IMA
  • Constanta
  • Perfecta
  • Maisa
  • HST, and more.

No matter the machine, we have the thread that works seamlessly for your operations.

Certified for Food Safety and Quality

HMC ensures all its products meet the highest food safety standards. The company holds several prestigious certifications, including:

  • ISO 22000:2018 (Food Safety Management)
  • GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) certified by SGS
  • Microbiological Test Certificates and other food safety tests

You can trust that the thread you receive is safe and fully compliant with international standards for food packaging.
