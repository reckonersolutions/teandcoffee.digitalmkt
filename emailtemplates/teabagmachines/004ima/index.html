About the IMA C21

The C21 produces non-heatsealable, double-chamber bags with string, folded tag, and aluminium staple. Reliable and efficient, the C21 is a highly automated, compact machine that can be completed with a host of optional units.

Production Speed:

  • With naked bag and crimped outer envelope: up to 180 bags/min
  • With heatsealed outer envelope: up to 150 bags/min
