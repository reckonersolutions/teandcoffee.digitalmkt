MSA TYPE TEAR TAPES

Monoaxially-oriented polypropylene or MOPP is our preferred base film for our MSA type tear tapes.

SA TYPE TEAR TAPES

The base film of our SA tear tapes is polyester (PET).

HM TYPE TEAR TAPES

Our HM tear tape is a type of adhesive coated tape that requires heat between 125C to 135C to attach to the wrapping film.
