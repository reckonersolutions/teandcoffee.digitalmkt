Maisa EC/12B Tea Bag Machine Available for Sale

Dear valued customer,

Reckoner Solutions is excited to offer the Maisa EC/12B, a high-performance automatic tea bag packaging machine, to tea factories around the world. The Maisa EC/12B is known for its reliability and efficiency in producing single-chamber tea bags with string and tag.

Machine Features:

  • Speed: Up to 140 tea bags per minute
  • Bag Type: Single chamber with crimped paper envelope
  • Packaging: The teabag is automatically wrapped with a paper protection, and the envelopes are piled, counted.
  • European Compliance: Meets all European Community standards for safety and efficiency.
